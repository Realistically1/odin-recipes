ingredient for Kare

  • 4 potatoes
  • a box of golden curry mix
  • Chuck roost beef, leaned
  • 4 carrots
  • Water

instruction

  1. season meat
  2. dice veggie
  3. salute meat in high heat using oil
  4. after 5 min in add the vegtable
  5. salute them to your liking and stir
  6. when the meat is browned, add in water or beef broth and simmer until boil
  7. after 15 min add your curry mix, add cube until thick
  8. enjoy!
